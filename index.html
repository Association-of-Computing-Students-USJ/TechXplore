What is TechXplore?

TechXplore is an initiative organized by the Association of Computing Students University of Sri Jayewardenepura. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry by delivering a dynamic seminar series that explores various technological areas.

We bring together industry professionals, experts and thought leaders to share their knowledge and experiences with students and enthusiasts. Through our seminars, we aim to inspire innovation, foster networking opportunities, and provide valuable insights into the ever evolving world of technology.
